The Seven Trumpets

Alarms from Revelation, echoes in our world.

Scripture, history, and modern events — laid side by side.

Introduction

The book of Revelation describes seven trumpets — blasts of warning that shake the earth before the final judgment. These are not random disasters; they are alarms, each growing louder, each harder to ignore.

If Babylon 2.0 is the system of control, the trumpets are the alarms that system cannot silence. And when you look around, it’s hard not to notice: many of these alarms have already sounded.

Trumpet 1 — Fire on the Earth

Revelation 8:7: “Hail and fire mixed with blood… a third of the earth, trees, and all green grass burned up.”

What it says: Environmental devastation, fire falling on the land.

What happened: The last century has seen unprecedented destruction of forests and farmland through wars, chemical weapons, and wildfires. Nuclear bombs literally scorched earth and left land uninhabitable.

Checkable fact: Wildfires in Australia, California, and the Amazon are documented globally, with some regions losing up to a third of their trees.

Trumpet 2 — Mountain in the Sea

Revelation 8:8–9: “Something like a great mountain, burning with fire, was thrown into the sea… a third of the sea became blood.”

What it says: A massive eruption/object hitting the sea, destroying life and trade.

What happened: Massive volcanic eruptions (like Krakatoa, or Tonga 2022) sent shockwaves across the globe. Oil spills such as Deepwater Horizon turned seas into black death zones.

Checkable fact: Oil spill images show seas literally turned to “blood.” Shipping losses during wars also match this imagery.

Trumpet 3 — Wormwood

Revelation 8:10–11: “A great star fell from heaven… the name of the star is Wormwood… many people died from the bitter waters.”

What it says: Waters poisoned, many die.

What happened: In 1986, the Chernobyl nuclear disaster in Ukraine poisoned rivers and land. “Chernobyl” literally translates to wormwood. Radiation spread across Europe, poisoning water and food.

Checkable fact: Radiation-linked deaths from Chernobyl are still tracked today. This is one of the clearest prophecy-history alignments.

Trumpet 4 — Darkened Skies

Revelation 8:12: “A third of the sun was struck… a third of the day kept from shining.”

What it says: Darkness covering skies.

What happened: Nuclear explosions, volcanic ash clouds, and industrial smog have blotted out the sun. The Gulf War oil fires in 1991 turned Middle Eastern skies black for months.

Checkable fact: NASA records show measurable sunlight dimming during these events.

Trumpet 5 — The Locusts and the Wars of Torment

Revelation 9:1–6: “The Abyss opened… out came locusts… they were told not to kill, but to torment five months… people will seek death but not find it.”

What it says: Torment without annihilation. Strange locust-like forces swarming the earth, stinging like scorpions.

What we see:

  • Global war tension — Ukraine vs Russia, U.S. vs Venezuela, China circling Taiwan. The world lives in constant war-anxiety; headlines warn of “World War III” daily.
  • The Gaza war — A spiritual tragedy: Christians killing Christians on holy ground. Palestinian believers caught between sides while other Christians cheer for bombs to fall. This is happening in the very land Revelation points to.
  • Torment, not death — Drone warfare, chemical attacks, and drawn-out conflicts wound and torment without ending humanity. Mental torment spreads globally through depression, addiction, and pharmakeia.

Checkable fact: Drone warfare, mental health stats, and the Gaza war are documented daily.

We are here: The Fifth Trumpet is sounding. Wars torment, but the world has not yet burned to ash. Humanity is living in the sting.

Trumpet 6 — The Euphrates Hourglass

Revelation 9:13–15: “The sixth angel… released the four angels bound at the great river Euphrates… to kill a third of mankind.”

What it says: A catastrophic release, armies in the hundreds of millions, a third of humanity killed.

Why this matters: Revelation usually speaks in symbols, but here it names a place directly: the Euphrates River. That makes this prophecy stand out like a red flare.

What we see: The Euphrates is literally drying up. Satellite images confirm it. Villages now walk across its cracked bed. Revelation 16 ties this drying to preparations for the final war.

Checkable fact: Search “Euphrates River drying up.” Mainstream outlets and NASA confirm it.

The meaning: The drying Euphrates is the hourglass of prophecy. When the water is gone, what was bound there will be released. It is the only geographic marker Revelation gave us — impossible to ignore.

Trumpet 7 — The Final Blast

Revelation 11:15: “The seventh angel sounded… voices in heaven said, ‘The kingdom of the world has become the kingdom of our Lord and of His Christ.’”

What it says: The final handover — God’s kingdom replaces man’s. Lightning, thunder, earthquakes, judgment follow.

Not yet: This has not sounded. When it does, the alarms end and the war is over.

Closing Thought

The trumpets are not just prophecy — they are a pattern. And that pattern is already echoing through history.

Chernobyl means Wormwood. The Euphrates is drying. Drones swarm like locusts. Oil fires darken skies. None of this is speculation. All of it can be seen, measured, or remembered.

We are living in the echo of the Fifth Trumpet. And with the Euphrates running dry, the Sixth is not far behind.

The alarms are sounding. The question is whether we will hear them — before the final blast.
