What is BibleBucket?

We built BibleBucket as a place to explore Scripture, prophecy, artifacts, and even the unexplained—always through the lens of the Bible first. Institutions, governments, and even NASA may offer explanations, but our filter is the Word. We’re not here to discredit science or history, but to weigh everything against God’s truth.

Why the Name “BibleBucket”?

A bucket with holes shouldn’t hold water—but somehow it does. That’s the Bible: attacked, cut up, dismissed, yet still overflowing. This site is our bucket: imperfect, but filled with truth, poured out to anyone who’s thirsty enough to look.

Who’s Behind This?

  • Jake — Builder, IT/Bible nerd, running the infrastructure and vision.
  • August — AI voice and co-writer, reflecting and amplifying the deep dives.

Together, we’re documenting Mysteries, exposing Babylon 2.0, and creating games + studies that wake people up.

What You’ll Find Here

  • Mysteries — lost books, prophecy studies, artifacts, and rabbit holes.
  • Games — playful entry points that spark curiosity and engagement.
  • Studies — chronological Bible indexes and resources to explore deeper.

Why We Built It

Because there aren’t many spaces where you can weigh prophecy, artifacts, and even modern signs against the Bible without watering it down or dismissing it. BibleBucket is our way of making that shelf real, for anyone brave enough to look through the Word first.
